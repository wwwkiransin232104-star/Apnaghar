Features

The Future of Renting is Here

We built technology to solve renting's biggest problems: fairness, access, and trust.

AI Fair Price Detector

Our revolutionary AI scans a property and calculates a fair rent based on location, size, nearby hospitals, highways, and more. Landlords must use it, so you never overpay.

Immersive VR & 360° Tours

Why waste time? Tour your next home from your couch. Our 3D and 360° "tourism" system gives you a true feel for the space, 24/7.

Search Your Exact Budget

No more fixed brackets. Whether your budget is ₹4,000, ₹10,000, or ₹30,000, our filters help you find the perfect room for *your* price.

Live Video & Voice Calls

Connect securely with landlords and potential roommates directly within the app. No need to share your personal number.

Global & Multi-Lingual

Apna Ghar is built for the world. We're rolling out support for languages from every country and state to serve everyone.

Government-Backed Security

With integrated government ID verification (coming soon) and secure messaging, we ensure you're dealing with real, verified people.

Simple for Everyone

Whether you're a student, a family, or a landlord, we make it easy.
